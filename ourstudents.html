WHO IS SERVED BY HOLLYWOOD ARTS?
Hollywood Arts provides curriculum-based, dedicated, sequential arts therapy and arts training for homeless, runaway and at-risk youth age 15 and older living either on the streets or in temporary shelters in Hollywood.

Our students are emancipated foster care teenagers, abuse victims and/or runaways.
